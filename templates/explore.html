About Us

Founded in 2022 in the heart of Kathmandu, Everest Provisions blends ancient traditions with modern techniques to produce high-quality, nutritious foods. Our mission is to promote sustainable agricultural practices and support local farmers while offering consumers unique, healthful food options

Product Highlight: Himalayan Gundruk

Himalayan Gundruk is made from organically grown mustard greens, radish leaves, and cauliflower leaves. These are carefully selected and fermented using traditional methods passed down through generations. The result is a tangy, probiotic-rich superfood that is packed with vitamins A, C, and K, as well as essential minerals like calcium and iron.

This content is a little bit longer.Gundruk is a traditional fermented leafy vegetable dish from Nepal, also enjoyed in parts of India such as Sikkim and Darjeeling. It is typically made from the leaves of mustard, radish, cauliflower, or other greens, which are wilted, shredded, and then fermented. The fermentation process involves packing the leaves in an earthenware container, adding warm water, and allowing the natural bacteria on the leaves to initiate fermentation.
